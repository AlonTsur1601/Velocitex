## Narration notes

- Record with a natural English-speaking human voice or a voice solution whose public-use rights are verified.
- Keep music and in-game sound below the narration; duck or mute it when the spoken evidence section begins.
- Do not claim exact development dates, token counts, or test results in narration unless the matching on-screen evidence is readable and current.
- Keep the on-screen code and Git views readable for at least two seconds each; never expose private paths, account information, or unrelated chat content.

No gameplay capture, voice recording, thumbnail, or final edit should begin until the creator approves this storyboard and narration.
